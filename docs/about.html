Our analysis pipeline uses Google's Gemini Flash LLM to systematically extract methodology sections from RAND documents:

1. Document Processing
Documents are chunked into manageable segments and analyzed by document type priority (RR â†’ MG â†’ TR â†’ PE â†’ WR â†’ etc.)
2. LLM Analysis
Gemini Flash analyzes each document with specialized prompts to identify methodology sections, research approaches, and analytical frameworks

4. Expert Mapping
Authors are mapped to their methodological expertise based on frequency and recent usage of specific research methods

Data Infrastructure

  • create_temporal_methodology_analysis.py - Trend analysis over time
