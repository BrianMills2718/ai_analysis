    1. Term Extraction
  • Hierarchical dictionary with 266 AI terms and 160+ policy methods

    2. LLM Classification (Gemini)
  • Model: gemini-1.5-flash
  • Classification: usage type, sophistication, AI integration
  • Parallel processing: 20 concurrent workers
  • Sample size: 10,000 contexts analyzed

    3. Location Quotient Analysis
